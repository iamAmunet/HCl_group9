Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What are the museum’s opening hours?

The museum is open from 9:00 to 16:30, Everyday. We are closed on public holidays only.

Is there an entrance fee?

Yes, Residents is K10 for adults, K5 for children, and for non-residents for adults $5 and children $3. Discounts are available for groups and schools.

Do you offer guided tours?

Absolutely! We offer guided tours daily at 10:00 AM, 12:00 PM, and 3:00 PM. Please book in advance for group tours.

Can I take photographs inside the museum?

Photography is allowed in most areas of the museum, but flash and tripods are not permitted. Certain exhibits may restrict photography.

Is the museum accessible for people with disabilities?

Yes, the museum is wheelchair accessible and we provide assistance for visitors with special needs. Please contact us in advance.
