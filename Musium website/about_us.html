About Us

Welcome to the Zambian Museum, a hub of history, culture, and discovery. Our mission is to preserve and showcase the rich heritage of our people through carefully curated exhibits, educational programs, and engaging experiences. Since our founding in 1938, we have been committed to inspiring curiosity and deepening the public's understanding of art, science, and history.

Who We Are

At the Zambian Museum, we believe every artifact has a story to tell. From ancient tools to modern marvels, our exhibits bring the past to life for visitors of all ages. We're more than just a museum - we're a community space where culture, learning, and imagination come together.
